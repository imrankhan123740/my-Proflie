MEGA

Rated for use in automotive electrical systems operating at up to 120 V, MEGA bolt-down fuses provide ideal overcurrent and short circuit protection solutions for batteries, alternators, and heavy gauge wire harnesses that experience large inrushes of current. Littelfuse MEGA fuses employ diffusion pill technology to provide predictable time-delayed performance, and the fuses can be used in EVs as well as gas, diesel, and hybrid vehicles.

fretures
  • Rated at 32 V dc, 70 V dc, and 120 V dc
  • Available with ampere ratings from 60 A to 650 A
  • Operate in temperatures ranging from –40 ℃ to +125 ℃
  • Copper or copper alloy terminals with tin or silver plating
  • RoHS compliant
Benefits
  • Available with two, one, or no mounting holes
  • High-contrast, color-coded ampere rating stamps on housings aid identification
